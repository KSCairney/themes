About Me

I am an archaeologist who discovered a love for web development. My experiences and studies have led me all over the world, and I have had the pleasure of meeting some amazing people. Now I have found a field that fulfills my quest to always be learning something new. I continue Archaeology as a hobby, while I pursue web development and new projects professionally.


This portfolio was built to tie together my archaeological interests and highlight my more recent explorations into web development. If any of my projects spark your interest, or if you would like to discuss any future projects, please contact me.
